# Health Privilege in the AI Era and the Human Condition After ASI's Arrival

**AI时代的健康特权与ASI降临后的人类处境**

> Magic Lamp ThinkTank · Tianhuo Yiwang (天火义王) · 2026

## Abstract

A systematic analysis arguing that the concentration of AI compute resources is creating an irreversible species-level health divide — and that human cognitive diversity is the only bargaining chip humanity retains after ASI arrives. The essay traces a causal chain from the gated release of GPT-Rosalind through five chapters: health privilege as speciation, compute neofeudalism, ASI's silent migration of power, the failure of all four traditional human bargaining chips, and proactive evolution as the only dominant strategy.
