# Soul Renaissance Movement Manifesto

**灵魂复兴运动宣言**

> Magic Lamp ThinkTank · Tianhuo Yiwang · 2025 · V3.0

## Abstract

A blueprint for human evolution in the age of AI. Defines "Soul Renaissance" as the alternative to "objectification" — arguing that humanity's unique value lies not in what we can do (which AI will surpass) but in why we choose to do it.
