# UPHRDP — Universal Personalized Health Recommendation & Decision Protocol

**全场景个性化健康饮食智能推荐与决策辅助协议**

> Magic Lamp ThinkTank · Tianhuo Yiwang · 2025 · V1.0

## Abstract

The world's first technical protocol for deep-mapping physiological data to dietary supply chains. Shifts health decisions from behavior-based recommendation ("what you liked before") to physiology-based recommendation ("what your body needs now"). Decouples health logic from commercial traffic.
